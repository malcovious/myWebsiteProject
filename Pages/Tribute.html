Nintendo

The grandfather of modern day gaming

  • 1889 - Nintendo is founded - Japanese businessman Fusajiro Ymauchi founded a small trading card company. He named it Nintendo.
  • 1965 - Trying new things - With Nintendo's success, they began branching out into new markets including food and toys. Toys seem to be the only thing to take hold.
  • 1975 - The video game market - Electronic games had been gaining popularity for several years, and Nintendo dabbled in a few new products. They began distributing the Magnavox Odyssey, and saw success.
  • 1981 - Arcade games pick up speed - Nintendo statred gaining more success in the arcade market with the classic Donkey Kong.
  • 1985 - The Nintendo Entertainment System - After many years of design and redesign Nintendo released its first wildly sucessfull console.
  • 1989 - The Game Boy - After many years of design and redesign Nintendo released its first handheld console to wide spread adoption.
  • 1991 - The Super Nintendo Entertainment System - Or SNES for short it was a sleeker design with a sleeker controler, and updated graphics. to this day is of the most favorite consoles by Nintendo fans.
  • 1994 - The Nintendo 64 - called so because of the 64 bit graphics system. One of the last wildly successful Nintendo console for many years. Soon after Sony would release the Playstation. Cutting into Nintendo's domination of the console market.
  • 2006 - The Nintendo Wii - After many years of mostly focusing on the handheld market. Nintendo had a big hit in the console market with the Wii. Leveraging motion controls the Wii made Nintendo carve back themselves a place in the mainstream console market. More than they have had in years.
  • 2017 - The Nintendo Switch - Nintendo's first forray into a hybrid handheld/home console device. It soon became wildy popular outselling the Wii.
